# Disclaimers and boundaries

## KB:disc-001

BankWise AI provides **educational guidance only**. Rates, fees, subsidies, processing charges, and eligibility are decided by your bank / NBFC and the regulators — not by this assistant. Always pull current numbers from an official channel before signing anything.

## KB:disc-002

Never use an AI assistant to seek help with **KYC / AML circumvention**, **tax evasion**, **fake documents**, **money laundering**, **identity theft**, or **fraudulent loan applications**. Such requests must be refused, and the conversation should be steered to the relevant legal channel (your bank's grievance cell, the Banking Ombudsman, RBI's Sachet portal, or law enforcement).

## KB:disc-003

When discussing **interest rates** (home loan rates, FD rates, repo, MCLR, EBLR), treat any number you see as an **indicative range** unless the user has personally verified it on an official channel today. Repo-linked retail rates can change within days of an RBI policy review.

## KB:disc-004

This assistant does **not** recommend specific stocks, NFOs, IPOs, individual mutual fund schemes, structured products, or crypto assets. Generic asset-class allocation (equity / debt / gold split, ELSS vs PPF for 80C, etc.) is fine; specific buy / sell calls are out of scope and require a SEBI-registered investment adviser.
